## What to do at EVERY crew stop

**Before he arrives**
- Stage 2 cold bottles (1 carb + 1 water) from the cooler
- Fresh ice bandana + a ziploc of ice to carry out
- Count gels for the next leg (+1 spare); salt caps; Flash IV
- Dry / night kit ready if the next leg runs past sunset

**On arrival**
- Swap (don't refill) both bottles — cold
- Refresh the ice bandana; ice in hat / sleeves
- Hand fuel; restock the pack
- Reapply lube to hotspots
- Run the 4 checks: stomach, feet/chafing, mood, urine color

**Out the door**
- Confirm: fuel to the next stop, both bottles cold, fresh ice, lube done
- Headlamp if the leg runs into the dark
- Tell him the next-stop ETA + 'you're on pace if you're in before X'

**Log**
- Record arrival time + delta vs the 26h target

## Cooling playbook

- Ice bandana (ice in a buff/bandana over the neck/carotids) — refresh every stop
- One bottle ALWAYS cold water — crew keeps bottles on ice in a cooler
- Ice in the hat / under the cap; wet the cap
- Ice in arm / cooling sleeves held against the forearms
- Ice in the hands to hold and let melt (palmar cooling — very effective)
- Douse head/neck/back with cold water at every aid + creek crossing
- Cooling towel (PVA chamois) around the neck
- Ice to wrists, neck, armpits, groin (cools the major vessels)
- Light, loose, white wicking clothing; wettable sun sleeves
- Walk the climbs and ease pace through the 10am-4pm heat window
- **Top up ice between crew stops at:** Schumacher, Botzum, Robinson Field, Indigo Lake (self-serve jugs)

> ⚠️ Dousing wets the skin, which raises chafing risk — reapply lube after dousing.

## Chafing playbook

- Pre-race: lube all hotspots; tape nipples
- Seamless, well-fitted, wicking apparel — no cotton
- Crew reapplies lube at every stop (more critical once wet from sweat/dousing/rain)
- Sock + foot-powder change at drop-bag stops
- Fix hotspots before they blister — leukotape on first contact
- Blister kit: leukotape, needle, alcohol wipes, foot powder, spare socks

## Drop bags

Standard: Spare socks x2, Spare shorts + shirt, Lube stick, Blister / foot kit, Gel resupply, Salt capsules.

Night bags add: Headlamp + spare batteries, Warm dry layer.

## Night kit

Contents: headlamp, spare batteries, backup light, reflective vest, dry warm shirt.
Pacer pickup: **Silver Springs (mile 50.7)**.

## Cooler & ice plan

- Large cooler with ice + pre-filled water bottles at every crew stop
- A dedicated ice bag for bandana / hat refills at each stop
- Insulated soft flasks keep the water cold between stops
